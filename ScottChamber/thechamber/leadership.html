Character Counts Program

Elementary schools in Scott County will soon be hosting skits, created and performed by students, which demonstrate the six pillars of character. A one-time grant from the Community Foundation has made this production possible.

In the skit, a group of students are trying to teach two aliens how people act on earth. They sing a song with six original verses to show the aliens how to use good character. Scotty Dog, the Scott County Mascot, will also have a role in the play. (CONTINUED)
